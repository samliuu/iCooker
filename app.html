About

(WARNING: USER MUST DOWNLOAD APP BEFORE PLUGGING THE PHONE INTO THE PORT) When the user plugs the phone into the phone port, the user will not be able to use the phone as long as it is plugged into the port. The control panel will light up and display the contents in the app. The control pannel will display a navigation bar for recipes and tips.

Recipe: Each recipe has a list of steps on how to make the meal. It will show the user how to cook the meal step by step which makes it very simple to understand. The app will tell the user what steps the user has to perform and it will also show which steps the iCooker will perform. Each meal can also tell the user how many nutrients are in it.

Tips: It will give information about how many calories are in the meal and the daily calorie intake for that specific meal. It can also share the amount of exercises in hours are needed to burn off the meal.
